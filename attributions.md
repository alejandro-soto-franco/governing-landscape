# Data attributions

Third-party datasets used by this project, with the citation each provider
requires. Binaries live outside the repository (see each site's
`data/sites/<site>/manifest.toml`); only manifests and READMEs are tracked.

## Great Mosque, Kilwa Kisiwani (reference heritage capture)

Used as a methodological reference dataset: a dense, well-overlapping
terrestrial-LiDAR plus aerial-photogrammetry capture that exercises the
COLMAP to gsplat reconstruction pipeline (Layers III and IV) end to end on
real survey data. See `data/sites/great-mosque-kilwa/`.

Please include the following citation when referencing this dataset:

> CyArk 2020: Great Mosque - Kilwa Kisiwani - LiDAR - Terrestrial,
> Photogrammetry - Terrestrial, Photogrammetry - Aerial. Distributed by
> Open Heritage 3D. https://doi.org/10.26301/bfzm-v295

- Provider: CyArk, in partnership with Google Arts and Culture, ICOMOS, and
  Tanzania's Ministry of Natural Resources and Tourism.
- Distributor: Open Heritage 3D, https://openheritage3d.org/project/bfzm-v295
- Collection date: 11 to 19 December 2018.
- Location: Kilwa Kisiwani, Tanzania (UNESCO World Heritage Site).
- License: CC BY-NC-SA (non-commercial, share-alike). Confirm the version on
  the Open Heritage 3D project page before any redistribution.

The non-commercial clause governs reuse: this dataset supports research and
method development here and is not redistributed in the repository.

## Ojúbo Òsogbo, Osun-Osogbo Sacred Grove (reference heritage capture)

A second methodological reference dataset alongside the Great Mosque at Kilwa:
a dense terrestrial-LiDAR plus aerial- and terrestrial-photogrammetry capture of
a UNESCO World Heritage cultural landscape, which exercises the COLMAP to gsplat
reconstruction pipeline (Layers III and IV) end to end on real survey data. See
`data/sites/ojubo-osogbo/`.

Please include the following citation when referencing this dataset:

> CyArk, ímísí3D 2020: Ojúbo Òsogbo - LiDAR - Terrestrial, Photogrammetry -
> Aerial, Photogrammetry - Terrestrial. Distributed by Open Heritage 3D.
> https://doi.org/10.26301/wr06-mh92

- Provider: CyArk, in partnership with ímísí3D.
- Distributor: Open Heritage 3D, https://openheritage3d.org/project/wr06-mh92
- Aerial imagery captured: 21 to 22 September 2019.
- Location: Osun-Osogbo Sacred Grove, Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria (UNESCO World
  Heritage Site, inscribed 2005).
- License: CC BY-NC-SA (non-commercial, share-alike). Confirm the version on the
  Open Heritage 3D project page before any redistribution.

The non-commercial clause governs reuse: this dataset supports research and
method development here and is not redistributed in the repository.
